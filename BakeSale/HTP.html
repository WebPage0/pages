How To Play Bake Sale


Setup:


1st place the cards in the center of your play area or board.

Then get a piece of paper to keep track of everyones scores.

How to play:


(Each person will have a turn) go clockwise when its a players turn they will draw a card and place it in their area, the goal is to get to 100 points the last person to 100 points loses.



About The Cards:


The cards have many diffrent outcomes such as:


1. Success Card - This card gives you 5 points.


2. The "Yuck!" Card - This card remove 5 points.


The lucky Card - This card gives you 10 points but in addition lets you avoid any card that removes points (this is only effective for 1 card).


Sabotage Card - A player you pick has to give you 5 points.


blessed - give someone besides you 5 points (does not come out of your points)

A player can trade their card with someone but the player that wanted to trade will have their turn skipped next time.


Have Fun!
